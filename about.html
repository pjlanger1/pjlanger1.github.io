about transit ventures and bikewatch

transit ventures is a new york city-based non-profit organization dedicated to bridging the gap between mass transit policy-makers and policy stakeholders using open data and an open-source analytics development philosophy. our mission is to improve social outcomes through research on public transit infrastructure use, with a special interest in ecologically sustainable micromobility solutions.

our research network brings together citizens, mobility advocates, engineers, and social scientists who collaborate to gather, analyze, and share data related to bike usage, public transit patterns, and urban mobility trends. through our projects and partnerships, we aim to foster a community of informed citizens and policymakers to help design our cities of tomorrow.

our mission

our mission is to empower communities with data and insights to create more effective and equitable transportation solutions. by leveraging technology and open data, we work to bridge the gap between transit users and service providers, advocating for sustainable and people-centered urban mobility.
